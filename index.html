Welcome!


This project is about data visualization for vision difficulty, ambulatory difficulty, hearing difficulty and cognitive difficulty.

The maps show the data for each state on click with three key values:

  1. Yes:
    Percentage of people with the difficulty
  2. No:
    Percentage of people without the difficulty
  3. NA:
    Percentage of people not sure

To access the maps, click on the link below to go to the page with the project maps

    Instructions for accessing the projects after opening the link:


    To access the maps, click on the buttons. On click, the maps are displayed on below.


    To get data for each state, click on the state. The data will be readily available on the alert box above the map.
